About us!

Our story

KBU Notes is a passion project originally submitted as a student end of year project but that will continue to develop to help students in KBU. I saw that students were suffering with sharing resources, they know that unity and cooperation will help them but they don’t have the means to do it. And how can we blame them! There is no centralized platform for them to share their notes and efforts, should they share on WhatsApp? Discord? Telegram? Face to face? Im sure you see the problem here… and this is where KBU NOTES comes in. To be the singular true centralized platform for all KBU students to share notes

Why use KBU NOTES

One location for all your notes

You don't have to keep runing after your friends to find location of notes, everything in a single easy to access system

Easily add and control your notes

Have notes that would help other students? Now you can share them with your friends easily.

Quicker and easier studying

Remember all the hours that you used to waste running after notes from doctors and students and jumping from application to application? Now you can invest these hours in actually studying the notes, not looking for them!
